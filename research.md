## Setup Guides

### MonitoRSS Setup
1. **Invite the Bot:** Visit [top.gg/bot/733963412631906365](https://top.gg/bot/733963412631906365) and click "Invite"
2. **Select Server:** Choose the Discord server where you want to add the bot
3. **Configure Permissions:** Ensure the bot has "Send Messages" and "Manage Webhooks" permissions
4. **Set Up Feeds:** Go to [monitorss.xyz](https://monitorss.xyz/) and add your RSS feeds
5. **Configure Rules:** Set up posting schedules and filters
6. **Test:** Post a test feed item to verify everything works

### NewsBot Setup
1. **Visit Top.gg:** Go to [top.gg/bot/473221602067087380](https://top.gg/bot/473221602067087380)
2. **Click Invite:** Select your server and authorize the bot
3. **Configure Channel:** Set the bot to post in your desired channel
4. **Use Commands:** Type `/news` in your Discord channel to get news updates

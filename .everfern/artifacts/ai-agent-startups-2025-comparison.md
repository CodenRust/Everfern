## Key Insights

1. **Massive Funding Acceleration**: AI Agent startups are raising larger rounds sooner than traditional SaaS. Cognition went from $1M to $73M ARR in ~9 months.

2. **Billion-Dollar Valuations Common**: Even early-stage AI agents (seed, Series A) are reaching $1B+ valuations rapidly.

3. **Customer Service Dominance**: Multiple top-funded companies focus on customer service automation (Sierra, Decagon, Wonderful).

4. **Coding Agents Hot**: Cognition ($10.2B) and Cursor/Anysphere ($9B) show massive investor interest in AI coding tools.

5. **Series A Bottleneck**: Only 57 Series A AI agent companies exist vs. 136 at seed stage — indicating a chokepoint in the funding funnel.

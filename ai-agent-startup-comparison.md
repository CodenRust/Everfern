## Detailed Profiles

### 1. Cognition Labs
- **Founded:** 2023 (San Francisco)
- **Product:** Devin - autonomous AI software engineer capable of end-to-end coding, debugging, and execution
- **Total Funding:** $600M+ 
- **Key Differentiator:** First AI agent to pass practical software engineering benchmarks; multi-agent collaboration (MultiDevin)
- **Competitive Moat:** Enterprise-grade security, full-task automation vs. code suggestions

### 2. Sierra
- **Founded:** 2022 (San Francisco)
- **Product:** Omnichannel conversational AI for customer service (chat, email, phone)
- **Total Funding:** $200M+
- **Key Differentiator:** Full-stack platform with knowledge engines for real-time routing; high-volume enterprise scalability
- **Competitive Moat:** Retail and telecom sector dominance; 100M+ conversations handled

### 3. Glean
- **Founded:** 2020 (Palo Alto)
- **Product:** Semantic knowledge graphs + AI agents for enterprise search, Q&A, onboarding, compliance
- **Total Funding:** $300M+
- **Key Differentiator:** Deep enterprise search integration; 100+ data source connectors
- **Competitive Moat:** Fortune 500 adoption; SOC2/HIPAA compliance for regulated industries

### 4. Hippocratic AI
- **Founded:** 2024 (Palo Alto)
- **Product:** Healthcare AI agents for patient intake, staffing optimization, non-diagnostic tasks
- **Total Funding:** $150M+
- **Key Differentiator:** AI Agent App Store; no-code tools for clinicians; safety-first approach
- **Competitive Moat:** HIPAA compliance; global healthcare staffing shortage addressable market

### 5. Decagon
- **Founded:** 2022 (San Francisco)
- **Product:** Conversational AI for customer service with Agent Operating Procedures
- **Total Funding:** $100M+
- **Key Differentiator:** Complex query handling; CRM integration; 40% resolution time improvement
- **Competitive Moat:** Enterprise automation focus; B2B SaaS integration depth

## Detailed Profiles

### 1. Cognition Labs — $10.2B Valuation | $400M Series C (Sep 2025)
- **Product:** Devin — the world's first autonomous AI software engineer
- **What it does:** Plans, writes, debugs, and deploys code end-to-end across entire software projects
- **Why it matters:** Represents the shift from code suggestions (copilots) to fully autonomous developers
- **Key metrics:** ARR grew from $73M to $155M in ~9 months; deployed at Goldman Sachs, Citi, Dell, Cisco
- **Lead investor:** Founders Fund
- **Team strength:** World-class founding team with deep ML research background

### 2. Sierra — $10B Valuation | $350M Raise (Sep 2025)
- **Product:** Omnichannel AI agent platform for customer service
- **What it does:** Deploys reasoning agents across chat, email, voice; handles complex queries autonomously
- **Why it matters:** Founded by Bret Taylor (Salesforce Co-CEO); reached $100M ARR in just 21 months
- **Key metrics:** $100M+ ARR; agents now reach 50%+ of U.S. families via healthcare and financial services
- **Lead investor:** Greenoaks Capital
- **Enterprise clients:** Discord, Rivian, SoFi, ADT, Cigna

### 3. Hippocratic AI — $3.5B Valuation | $126M Series C (Nov 2025)
- **Product:** Healthcare AI agent app store + no-code agent builder for clinical workflows
- **What it does:** Automates non-diagnostic patient tasks: intake, scheduling, staffing optimization
- **Why it matters:** Addresses global healthcare staffing shortages ($170B+ problem); HIPAA-compliant focus
- **Key metrics:** Valuation doubled from $1.64B (Jan 2025 Series B) to $3.5B (Nov 2025 Series C) in 10 months
- **Lead investor:** Avenir Growth
- **Differentiation:** Safety-first approach; built for clinical compliance from the ground up

### 4. Decagon — $1.5B Valuation | $131M Series C (Jun 2025)
- **Product:** Enterprise customer service AI with Agent Operating Procedures (AOPs)
- **What it does:** Structured agent frameworks for consistent, complex query resolution
- **Why it matters:** 40% reduction in resolution times; deep CRM integration; enterprise-scale deployment
- **Key metrics:** Strong adoption in enterprise support; valuation $1.5B post-Series C
- **Lead investor:** Bessemer Venture Partners

### 5. Adept AI — $1B Valuation | $350M Series B (Mid-2025)
- **Product:** Multimodal AI agents that interact with real enterprise software visually
- **What it does:** Parses software interfaces visually; automates workflows like data entry and logistics
- **Why it matters:** Positioned as the "automation layer" for enterprise software; real-world tool use
- **Key metrics:** $350M+ funding; strong enterprise automation use cases in supply chain and finance
- **Differentiation:** Visual parsing of interfaces (unlike text-only agents); real software interaction
